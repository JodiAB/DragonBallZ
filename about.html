Dragon Ball Z

Dragon Ball Z is a Japanese anime television series produced by Toei Animation. Part of the Dragon Ball media franchise,
it is the sequel to the 1986 Dragon Ball anime series and adapts the latter 325 chapters of the original Dragon Ball manga
series created by Akira Toriyama.

"Dragon Ball Z" follows the adventures of Goku who, along with the Z Warriors, defends the Earth against evil. The action
adventures are entertaining and reinforce the concept of good versus evil. "Dragon Ball Z" teaches valuable character virtues
such as teamwork, loyalty, and trustworthiness.
